About

Compass is a resource anyone can use to locate local Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) and Women, Infants, and Children (WIC) food and nutrition resources. Compass will tell you–in English or Spanish–whether you're likely to qualify and where to find the nearest office.
